## TF2Autobot GUI Panel v3.5.0

### Unlisted Stock page

New sidebar tab for tradable backpack items that are **not on your pricelist** yet.

- Highlights recent trade pickups from the last 7 days
- List items for sale individually or bulk-add with autoprice
- Requires bot inventory IPC support (see `patches/tf2autobot/INVENTORY_IPC.md`)
